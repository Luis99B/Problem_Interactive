Traffic congestion

Bad for everyone

Heavy traffic in big cities is an issue we have to deal with every day. The excess of motorized vehicles keeps us from getting to work in time, leaves a big carbon footprint, stresses our cars’ engines, increases chances of collision, and overwhelms even the best of us. There are other alternatives to cars that are able to lighten the streets, but must people don’t invest on them. What can we do?

It is a non-productive activity we deal with daily.

Delays when getting to work or home.

It leaves you with less leisure time because you take up extra time to get to your destination.

Increases fuel consumption of your vehicle due to continuous braking and acceleration.

Higher emission of harmful gases into the atmosphere.

Tight space between cars increases the possibility of road accidents.

It drives us crazy!
